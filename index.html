Town of Wellesley Engineering Division

Key workstreams written as portfolio projects.

Digital Inspection Workflow

What: Inspections relied on paper forms, which slowed turnaround and made data harder to share and track.

How: Built and implemented an ArcGIS based workflow using ArcGIS Online, Survey123, and Field Maps so inspections were captured consistently and accessible to the team.

Result: Faster inspection turnaround, improved consistency, and field data available to engineers immediately.

Python Automation for Drafting and Organization

What: Repetitive drafting and file organization tasks took significant time and made work harder to standardize.

How: Wrote multiple Python programs to automate drafting related steps and improve organization of engineering files and notes.

Result: Less time spent on repetitive work and more consistent output across the team.

Surveying and Plan Development Support

What: Projects required accurate site measurements for design and documentation.

How: Collected measurements using Trimble GPS and total station equipment and brought the data into CAD and GIS for plan development.

Result: Faster transition from field measurements to drawing updates and fewer gaps between site conditions and plans.

Utility Plan Updates and Conflict Flagging

What: Field conditions often differed from existing drawings and conflicts needed to be caught early.

How: Updated sewer, drainage, and utility plans based on field conditions and raised conflicts during review and inspection cycles.

Result: Issues were identified earlier and helped reduce rework during construction.
