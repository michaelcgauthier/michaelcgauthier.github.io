About

Engineer, builder, and systems thinker.

I’m a Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern University graduating in May 2027 with a minor in Entrepreneurial Startups. I’m drawn to work where details matter, the process has to hold up, and the final result has to be useful to other people.

My recent co-op with the Town of Wellesley Engineering Division included CAD plan production, field measurements with total station and Trimble GPS equipment, ArcGIS inspection system implementation, and Python programs that automated repetitive drafting and file organization work.

Outside of co-op, I’ve worked on engineering team projects involving electric racing, prosthetic hand design, clean-energy education, and product design through my custom LED sign business. I enjoy projects that connect design thinking, technical execution, and clear outcomes.
