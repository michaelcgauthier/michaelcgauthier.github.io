About

Wellesley case studies

Major workstreams from my co op

Digital inspection workflow

Problem

Inspections relied on paper forms, which slowed turnaround and made tracking and sharing harder.

What I did

Built and implemented an ArcGIS workflow using ArcGIS Online, Survey123, and Field Maps so inspections were recorded consistently and visible to the team.

Outcome

Faster inspection turnaround, improved consistency, and field data available to engineers sooner.

Python automation for drafting and organization

Problem

Repetitive drafting steps and file organization slowed work and made outputs harder to standardize.

What I did

Wrote multiple Python programs to automate drafting related steps and organize engineering notes and drawings more consistently.

Outcome

Less time spent on repetitive tasks and smoother handoffs when others needed to find and use files.

Survey measurements into plan development

Problem

Projects needed accurate site measurements that could be turned into drawings quickly.

What I did

Collected measurements using Trimble GPS and total station equipment and brought the data into CAD and GIS for plan work.

Outcome

Faster transition from field measurements to drawing updates and fewer gaps between site conditions and plan sets.

Utility plan updates and conflict flagging

Problem

Field conditions often differed from drawings and conflicts needed to be caught early.

What I did

Updated sewer, drainage, and utility plans based on field conditions and flagged conflicts during review and inspection cycles.

Outcome

Issues were identified earlier and helped reduce rework during construction.
