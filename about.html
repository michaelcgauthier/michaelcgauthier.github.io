About

Mechanical engineering student focused on design, systems, and results people can use.

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern University graduating May 2027 with a minor in Entrepreneurial Startups. I like understanding how systems work, improving them, and building tools that make technical work easier for a team to use every day.

Background

My first major design and manufacturing experience came from starting CA Light Designs, a custom engraved LED sign business I ran in high school. I designed products, operated and modified engraving equipment, built a website, managed online storefronts, communicated with customers, and fulfilled orders. That experience taught me how product design, manufacturing workflow, quality control, customer needs, and business operations connect.

More recently, I completed a co op with the Town of Wellesley Engineering Division where I worked across CAD design, field measurement, Python automation, GIS systems, and construction documentation. I created drawings, automated repetitive workflows, operated precision measurement equipment, processed field data, organized technical documentation, and built tools that improved how an engineering office works.

I am currently training for the Boston Half Marathon and I have organized fundraising events that raised about one thousand dollars for cancer research. I enjoy roles where I can stay organized, take initiative, and deliver work that is accurate and easy for others to rely on.
